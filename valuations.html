Arranging your Valuation

The current NI COVID restrictions allow for in-person evaluations (woohoo!), however we still love allowing our clients the ability to choose.

This is why our experts offer valuations through video conferencing, such as Skype and Whatsapp to ensure you can access this service in whatever way suits you best.

If you do choose an in-person valuation, our experts will sanitise before entry, socially distance and wear a face covering to keep you safe!

Valuation Report

Following your valuation, our experts will put together your free valuation report.

You will be presented with your estimated property value, as well as any other details including any answers to questions you may have asked during your valuation.
